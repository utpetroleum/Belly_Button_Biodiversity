From Bacteria to Beef

Want to find the answer to the old-age question, "Which bacteria make the best synthetic beef?” Find the answer here! 153 volunteers participated in Improbable Beef experiment to determine the bacterial species in their belly buttons. The top 10 bacterial species in their belly buttons are visually displayed below by their Test Subject ID No. If Improbable Beef identifies a species as a candidate to manufacture synthetic beef, volunteers will be able to identify whether that species is found in their navel.
